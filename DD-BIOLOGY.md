## B1: Self-Maintaining Structures

**Status:** FORCED (existence) / BOUND (specific cycles)

**DD interpretation:** Autocatalysis = self-reinforcing distinguishability pattern. The cycle maintains its own conditions for distinction.

## B4: Evolution

### Claim: Natural selection is forced (not a "theory")

**Required inputs:**
1. Replication with variation (B2 + noise)
2. Limited resources (thermodynamics)
3. Differential persistence

**Derivation:**
1. Replicators vary (copying errors inevitable)
2. Resources finite → not all copies survive
3. Survival correlates with stability (criticality)
4. Stable variants accumulate
5. This IS natural selection

**Status:** FORCED (mechanism)

**DD interpretation:** Evolution = trajectory through distinguishability space under criticality constraint. "Fitness" = proximity to critical regime.

**Key point:** Natural selection is not a biological hypothesis. It's a mathematical consequence of replication + variation + selection. Darwin discovered a theorem.

---

### Claim: Increasing complexity is not forced

**Analysis:**
1. Selection favors stability, not complexity
2. Sometimes simpler = more stable
3. Complexity increases only when it aids criticality

**Status:** NOT FORCED (complexity itself)

**DD interpretation:** Complexity is BOUND — constrained by criticality, not maximized. Parasites, bacteria often outcompete complex organisms.
